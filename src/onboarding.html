Welcome to Claude Usage Monitor
A few things to know before you get started.
✕
Hiding the widget
Clicking ✕ hides the widget to the system tray — it keeps running in the background. Click the tray icon near your clock to bring it back.
↔
Reposition anywhere
Drag the widget to any spot on your screen. It remembers its position between sessions and after restarts, so it's always where you left it.
☰
Tray icon menu
Right-click the tray icon near the clock for quick access to Show / Hide, Refresh, Settings, and Quit.
◎
Usage alerts
You'll get a Windows notification when your session or weekly usage gets high. Adjust the thresholds anytime in Settings.
